Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our handcrafted candles and services.

What kind of wax do you use in your candles?

We primarily use natural soy wax, known for its clean, long-lasting burn and excellent scent throw. Some specialized candles may use a blend of soy and other natural waxes like coconut wax for optimal performance.

Are your wicks lead-free?

Absolutely! All Goodlight candles feature lead-free cotton wicks, ensuring a safe and non-toxic burning experience.

How long do your candles burn?

Burn times vary depending on the candle size and type. On average, our standard 8oz jar candles burn for approximately 40-50 hours. Larger candles will have longer burn times.

What are phthalate-free fragrance oils?

Phthalates are chemical compounds sometimes used in fragrances. We exclusively use high-quality, phthalate-free fragrance oils to ensure our candles are safe for you and your home.

Do you offer custom candle orders or bulk purchases?

Yes, we do! For custom orders for events, gifts, or corporate branding, please contact us directly through our 'Contact Us' page. We also offer wholesale pricing for bulk purchases.

How should I care for my Goodlight candle?

For best results, trim the wick to 1/4 inch before each use. Allow the wax pool to reach the edge of the container on the first burn to prevent tunneling. Do not burn for more than 4 hours at a time. Keep away from drafts, children, and pets. Never leave a burning candle unattended.

What is your shipping policy?

We ship nationwide across Kenya. Orders are typically processed within 2-3 business days. Shipping costs and delivery times will vary based on your location and will be calculated at checkout.
